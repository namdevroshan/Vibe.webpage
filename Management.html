Streamline your healthcare organization's data management with our comprehensive master data management solution. Ensure data consistency, accuracy, and governance across all your systems and departments.

VIBE Master Data Management (MDM)

The VIBE Master Data Management (MDM) Tool is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline and centralize the management of critical healthcare data. Built to ensure data accuracy, consistency, and reliability, it empowers organizations to efficiently configure, manage, and govern master data across diverse healthcare systems.

VIBE comes with preconfigured real-time datasets such as Postcode, National Codes, General Practitioners (GPs), and GP Practices, which can be automatically synchronized with public datasets. In addition to data configuration, the tool offers robust data capturing and recording capabilities, along with features that support data stewardship and data cataloguing, ensuring that data governance policies are maintained effectively.

VIBE enables seamless integration with leading Electronic Patient Record (EPR) systems like Cerner, Epic, and Lorenzo, ensuring full interoperability with existing clinical workflows. It facilitates bidirectional data flow, allowing real-time updates between the VIBE platform and connected EPR systems, while ensuring compliance with HL7 and FHIR standards for secure and standardized data exchange. Additionally, VIBE integrates directly with NHS Digital services and analytics platforms, supporting national-level reporting, benchmarking, and data-driven decision-making.
